Site Intent

Signs by Design is a small South Carolina company that designs and manufactures all types of signage. They deal primarily on a local level, but are looking to expand nationally. Signs by Design pride themselves on being a one-stop shop for all your signage needs, including repairs and maintenance.

The website doesn't currently offer any pricing information and seems to be more of an informational page with very little interactivity. I am going to incorporate into the gallery page, images of past signage projects and an estimated price range based on materials used. I am also going to create more imagery on the home page that reaches out and grabs your attention.

The website was originally designed for desktop computers and only desktop computers. There is currently no responsive web design incorporated into it. I will make the website responsive to at least 3 different device sizes.
